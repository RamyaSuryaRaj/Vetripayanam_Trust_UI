We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

CONSERVATION OF NATURE

To promote and protect natural herbs and plants by cultivation, preservation, storage, processing, demonstration, creating botanical garden and providing necessary training.

To organize Sexuality Education camps and awareness programmes for Children and Adolescents to help them to prevent themselves from not being victim of sexual seduction, sexual harassment, and sexual abuse.
